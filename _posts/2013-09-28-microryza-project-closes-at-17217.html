--- layout: post title: "Microryza project closes at $17,217" author: admin date: 2013-09-28 12:00:00 ---

As of a few minutes ago, our first Microryza crowdfunding campaign has just officially finished.  We raised a total of $17,217, which is 215% of our original $8,000 goal and a few hundred more than even our highest “stretch goal”.

We will be in touch with lots more updates as things move forward.  But for now, it’s 3:00 am in Boston, and we just want to say: thank you.  You are all amazing.
