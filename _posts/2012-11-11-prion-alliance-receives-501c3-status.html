--- layout: post title: "Prion Alliance receives 501(c)(3) status" author: admin date: 2012-11-11 16:00:13 ---

Good news: in a Letter of Determination dated October 23, 2012, the Internal Revenue Service has granted 501(c)(3) tax exempt status to Prion Alliance, Inc.!  We are now a federally recognized non profit organization and your donation to us will be tax-deductible.  We are almost ready to receive online donations and will begin fundraising this month.
